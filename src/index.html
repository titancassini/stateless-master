Stateless

“A person who is not considered as a national by any State under the operation of its law” – The international legal definition of a stateless person

A social media network for the more than 12 million stateless people around the world. Stateless provides individual and collective digital identity, an opportunity build community, send remittances, make and vote on propositions, exchange socially valuable services, access support, fight corruption and abuse of human rights. Most of all, Stateless exists to support the dignity and lives of all people irrespective of states and borders.
